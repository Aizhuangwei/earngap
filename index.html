Methodology

7-Dimension Scoring System

Each opportunity is scored across 7 dimensions: Confidence, Timeliness, Executability, Asymmetry, Market Heat, Competition, and Scalability. The overall score (1-10) determines the conviction level.

Opportunity Window

We classify each opportunity by its likely execution window: <7 days (immediate), 7-30 days (short-term), 30-90 days (medium-term), and >90 days (long-term). Windows are updated daily based on market conditions.
